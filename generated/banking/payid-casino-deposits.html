What is PayID?

PayID is Australia's real-time instant payment system, launched in 2018 as part of the New Payments Platform (NPP) — a modern banking infrastructure co-built by the Reserve Bank of Australia and Australia's major financial institutions.

Instead of using a BSB and account number, PayID lets you link your bank account to a simple alias — your mobile phone number, email address, or ABN. To send money, the sender just enters your alias. Transfers settle in seconds, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year — including public holidays.

You open your banking app, send the deposit amount directly to that alias, and funds appear in your casino balance almost instantly. No e-wallet middleman, no card decline issues, no fees — just a clean, direct bank transfer.
