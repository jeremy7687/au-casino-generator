JILI Free Games Features Explained

One of the hallmarks of JILI pokies is how their free-games features are designed. Super Ace Free Games

In Super Ace, free games are triggered by landing bonus card symbols on the reels. Rather than a simple scatter trigger, the game uses a card-flip mechanic where each flip reveals a multiplier (1x through 5x) applied to your winning spins during the bonus. Three or more bonus symbols on any spin trigger the feature, and the multipliers stack — meaning a single free-games round can deliver 5x to 25x amplified wins. The feature can also re-trigger, extending your session without additional bets. This is what makes Super Ace especially popular among NSW and Victoria punters who want high-RTP gameplay with meaningful bonus depth.

Golden Empire Free Games

Golden Empire uses a cascade (tumble/avalanche) mechanic combined with free spins. When you trigger the bonus with 3 or more scatter symbols, winning combinations explode and new symbols fall into place — multiple consecutive wins are possible from a single spin. A multiplier meter rises with each cascade during free spins, and the feature can deliver win chains of 8–12x or more during a strong session. This cascading free spins system distinguishes Golden Empire from standard JILI titles and explains its 97.09% RTP, which ranks among the best online pokies Australia real money options offer.

Hold-and-Spin Mechanics

Several JILI titles — including JILI Caishen and Money Coming — use a hold-and-spin mechanic instead of traditional free spins. In these games, landing a set number of bonus symbols (typically 6+) locks those symbols in place and awards three re-spins. Any new bonus symbols that land during re-spins reset the counter back to three. The round ends when re-spins run out or the entire grid is filled with bonus symbols (triggering a jackpot payout).
