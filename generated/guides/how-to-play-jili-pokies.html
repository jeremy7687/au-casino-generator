Golden Empire Free Games

Golden Empire uses a cascading reel mechanic — winning symbol clusters explode and are replaced by new falling symbols, allowing chain reactions on a single spin. Three or more temple scatter symbols trigger the free games mode, and this is where the game's multiplier tier system activates. Each consecutive cascade win within a free spin upgrades the active multiplier: 1x → 2x → 3x → 5x. If the chain breaks, the multiplier resets, but it can be rebuilt within the same free games round. Golden Empire's 97.09% RTP combined with 6,000x maximum win makes it arguably the most balanced high-volatility JILI pokie — popular among VIC punters who want big-win potential without extreme variance swings.

Hold-and-Win Mechanics

Several JILI titles also incorporate hold-and-win or lock-and-spin features, which work differently from standard free spins. In these modes, landing a special symbol (usually a coin or bonus token) locks it in place while the remaining reels respin. The feature continues until no new qualifying symbols land, at which point the total of all locked coin values is paid out as a lump sum. JILI Caishen and Money Coming both use variants of this mechanic — delivering a more predictable-feeling bonus experience compared to the scatter-triggered free spin modes in Super Ace and Golden Empire.
