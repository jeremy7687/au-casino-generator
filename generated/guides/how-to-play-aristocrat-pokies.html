Reel Power vs Paylines: Understanding Aristocrat's Unique System

One of Aristocrat's most distinctive and influential contributions to pokie design is the Reel Power system — a ways-to-win mechanic that replaces traditional fixed paylines with all-ways-adjacent wins. Instead of winning along specific lines, any matching symbols on adjacent reels from left to right create a win. This means significantly more ways to win per spin and fundamentally changes how you size your bets.
