Reel Power vs Paylines: Aristocrat's Unique System

One of Aristocrat's most distinctive contributions to pokie design is the Reel Power system — a ways-to-win mechanic that replaces traditional fixed paylines with every possible left-to-right combination across adjacent reels. Understanding the difference between Reel Power and standard paylines is essential for making informed bet-sizing decisions at any online pokies Australia real money site.

In a traditional payline pokie like Queen of the Nile, you select how many of the fixed paylines (e.g. 1 to 20) you want to activate. Wins only pay on active lines. In contrast, Reel Power titles like Buffalo and 5 Dragons count all adjacent symbol combinations left-to-right — meaning every symbol on every reel can contribute to a win, regardless of position. Your total bet covers all ways automatically.
