🔢 Wagering Requirements Explained

Wagering requirements (also called playthrough requirements) are the most important factor in assessing the real value of any casino bonus. Here's everything you need to know before claiming a bonus at an Australian online casino.

When a casino offers you a bonus — whether that's a no deposit bonus, a welcome match, or free spin winnings — those funds come with a condition: you must wager (bet) the bonus amount a set number of times before you can withdraw real money. This is called the wagering or rollover requirement, and it's expressed as a multiplier like 30x, 35x or 45x.
