Booongo Hold & Win Explained

Hold & Win is Booongo's signature bonus mechanic — and understanding it is the key to getting the most from their pokies online. First pioneered by Playson and Booongo simultaneously in the mid-2010s, Booongo's implementation has become one of the most refined versions of the feature in the industry. It's a major reason why their pokies are attracting more real money action from punters across NSW, VIC, and Queensland.

How it works: During the base game, special coin or jackpot symbols appear on the reels. When 6 or more land in a single spin, the Hold & Win bonus round is triggered. You receive 3 re-spins. Every time a new coin symbol lands on the reels during re-spins, it locks in place and the counter resets to 3. The feature ends when either you run out of re-spins or all positions on the grid are filled — the latter awards the Grand Jackpot. Coin symbols carry fixed monetary values (Mini, Minor, Major, Grand) that are revealed when they land and collected at the feature's end.
