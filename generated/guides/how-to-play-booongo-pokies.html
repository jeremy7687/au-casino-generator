Booongo Hold & Win Explained

Hold & Win is a bonus game mechanic popularised in land-based pokies and brought to the online space by several developers — but Booongo's implementation is widely regarded by experienced punters as among the most refined available. Understanding how it works before you start playing can meaningfully improve your results.

The basic structure is consistent across all Booongo Hold & Win titles: land a minimum number of coin or special symbols during the base game (typically 6 on a 5×3 grid) to trigger the feature. You receive three re-spins. The standard reels are replaced by a grid where coin symbols stick in position and count toward your total. Landing any additional coin symbol during a re-spin resets the counter to three. The bonus ends when re-spins reach zero or the entire grid is filled with coins — the latter awarding the Grand Jackpot. Special high-value coins (Mini, Minor, Major, Grand) can appear at any position and are worth fixed cash multipliers.
