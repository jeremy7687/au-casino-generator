4.Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. AussiePokies96 and our third-party partners use the following types of cookies:

Session Cookies

Temporary cookies that exist only for the duration of your browsing session. These are used to maintain basic website functionality (for example, remembering your preferences within a single visit). Session cookies are deleted automatically when you close your browser.

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymised data about how visitors interact with the site. Google Analytics sets persistent cookies (typically lasting up to 2 years) to distinguish returning visitors and track sessions. We have enabled IP anonymisation, which means your full IP address is never stored by Google Analytics — the final octet is masked before data is recorded.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Affiliate Tracking Cookies

When you click a link on AussiePokies96 that directs you to a partner casino, the destination website may set its own cookies to track the referral source. These cookies are set by the third-party casino, not by us, and are subject to the casino's own privacy policy. These cookies allow the casino to recognise that you arrived via our affiliate link, which is how we earn commission.

We have no control over third-party affiliate tracking cookies once you leave our site.

Managing and Disabling Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Instructions for common browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions
Please note: Disabling or deleting cookies will not prevent you from accessing content on AussiePokies96. However, blocking affiliate tracking cookies means our partner casinos may not be able to credit us with a referral, which does not affect your experience with those casinos in any way.
