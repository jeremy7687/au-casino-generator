What Is PayID?

PayID is Australia's real-time bank transfer system, launched in 2018 and built on the New Payments Platform (NPP) — the same infrastructure that powers instant payments between all major Australian banks. Instead of sharing a BSB and account number, you register a simple alias: your mobile number, email address, or ABN. That alias becomes your PayID.

When you use PayID at an online casino, the deposit is routed through the NPP's real-time rails directly from your bank account to the casino's account in a matter of seconds — no third-party wallets, no credit card processing delays, no waiting overnight for funds to clear. Deposits are completely free, available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week including weekends and public holidays.

For pokies punters, PayID is the ideal deposit method because your BSB and account number are never exposed to the casino — only your alias is shared. The NPP is regulated and overseen by the Reserve Bank of Australia, making it one of the most secure domestic payment rails available.
