What is PayID?

PayID is Australia's real-time payment identifier, launched in February 2018 as part of the New Payments Platform (NPP) — the infrastructure built by Australia's banking sector and overseen by the Reserve Bank of Australia. Instead of sharing your BSB and account number, you register a simple alias — your mobile number, email address, or ABN — and link it to your bank account. Anyone who knows your PayID can send money directly to you in seconds, any time of day, any day of the year.

For online casino deposits, PayID is a game-changer. When you choose PayID at the cashier, the casino provides its own PayID alias. You open your banking app, send the desired amount to that alias, and your casino balance updates within seconds. There are no card numbers to expose, no third-party wallets to top up, and zero fees on either end. The NPP rails run 24/7, including public holidays — meaning your midnight Saturday deposit clears just as fast as a Tuesday afternoon one.
