What is PayID?

PayID is Australia's real-time bank transfer system, launched in 2018 and built on the New Payments Platform (NPP) — the modern payment infrastructure regulated by the Reserve Bank of Australia. Instead of entering your BSB and account number, you register a simple alias — your mobile number, email address, or ABN — and that becomes your PayID. Transfers happen in seconds, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, including weekends and public holidays.

For online casino deposits, PayID is a game-changer. When you fund your account, the casino provides its own PayID address. You open your banking app, send the amount to that address, and your casino balance updates almost immediately — no card details exposed, no third-party e-wallet account to manage, and absolutely no fees on either end. It's the closest thing to handing over cash, but with a complete digital paper trail and bank-grade security behind every transaction.

PayID is supported by every major Australian bank — CommBank, ANZ, NAB, Westpac, ING, Bendigo, Macquarie, St.George, Bank of Queensland, Suncorp — and 90+ credit unions and mutual banks. If you have an Australian bank account, you almost certainly have access to PayID already.

Pro tip: Complete your casino account KYC verification before your first PayID withdrawal. Most casinos process verified PayID payouts in under 5 minutes — unverified accounts face delays regardless of the payment method.
