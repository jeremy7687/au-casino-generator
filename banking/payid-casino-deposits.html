What Is PayID?

PayID is Australia's real-time bank transfer system, launched in 2018 as part of the New Payments Platform (NPP) — a national payments infrastructure regulated by the Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA). Instead of sharing your BSB and account number, you register a simple alias: your mobile number, email address, or ABN. Anyone can send money directly to that alias in seconds, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — including weekends and public holidays.
