What Is PayID?

Instead of sharing your BSB and account number, you register a simple alias — your mobile number, email address, or ABN — and that becomes your PayID.

When a casino provides you their PayID address, you simply open your banking app, send funds to that alias, and the money arrives in your casino account within seconds.
