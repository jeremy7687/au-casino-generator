Australian Gambling Law

The primary piece of legislation governing online gambling in Australia is the Interactive Gambling Act 2001 (Cth) ("IGA"), administered by the Australian Communications and Media Authority (ACMA). Users of this Site should be aware of the following legal context:

  • Under the IGA, it is unlawful for Australian-based operators to provide real-money interactive gambling services (including online pokies and casino games) to Australian residents.
  • However, there is no provision in the IGA that makes it illegal for Australian residents to access or play at offshore-licensed online casinos. The prohibition applies to operators, not players.
  • All online casinos listed and reviewed on AussiePokies96 are licensed in offshore jurisdictions such as Curaçao, Malta (MGA), Gibraltar, Kahnawake, or equivalent regulatory bodies. These casinos legally accept Australian players under their respective offshore licences.
