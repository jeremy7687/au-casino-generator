4.7 Australian Gambling Law

The primary legislation governing online gambling in Australia is the Interactive Gambling Act 2001 (Cth) (IGA), as amended. Under the IGA:

  • Australian-based operators are prohibited from offering certain interactive gambling services (including online casino games) to Australian residents.
  • The Australian Communications and Media Authority (ACMA) has powers to enforce these restrictions and block non-compliant offshore services.
  • However, it is not illegal under the IGA for Australian residents to access and use licensed offshore gambling websites.

All casinos listed and reviewed on AussiePokies96 are licensed in recognised offshore jurisdictions, which may include Curaçao, Malta (MGA), Isle of Man, Gibraltar, or equivalent regulatory bodies. These casinos legally accept Australian players under the laws of their licensing jurisdiction.
