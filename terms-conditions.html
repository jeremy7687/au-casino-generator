Australian Gambling Law

The primary federal legislation governing online gambling in Australia is the Interactive Gambling Act 2001 (Cth) ("IGA"). Under the IGA, it is an offence for an Australian-based operator to provide certain interactive gambling services (including real-money pokies and table games) to Australian residents without appropriate licensing.

However, the IGA does not make it unlawful for individual Australian residents to access or use licensed offshore gambling websites. Australians may legally use online casinos that are licensed and regulated in foreign jurisdictions.

All casinos listed and reviewed on AussiePokies96 are licensed in reputable offshore jurisdictions, including but not limited to Curaçao, Malta (MGA), Gibraltar, or equivalent regulatory bodies, and are available to Australian players under the existing legal framework.
