4.Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to function correctly and to collect anonymous usage data. A cookie is a small text file stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website.

4.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that exist only while your browser is open. They help the website function correctly during your visit (for example, maintaining a consistent browsing session) and are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics): We use Google Analytics to collect aggregated, anonymised data about how visitors use our site. Google Analytics uses cookies to track page views, session duration, traffic sources, and device information. We have enabled IP anonymisation in Google Analytics, meaning your full IP address is never stored by Google on our behalf. Data collected is statistical and not linked to identifiable individuals. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Affiliate Tracking Cookies: When you click an outbound link to a partner casino, that casino's website — or its affiliate platform — may set a tracking cookie on your device. This cookie records that you were referred from AussiePokies96 and is used to attribute any subsequent registration or deposit to our affiliate account. These cookies are set by third parties (the casino operators) and are governed by their own privacy policies. We do not control these cookies.

4.2 Managing & Disabling Cookies

You have the right to accept or refuse cookies. Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Here is how to manage cookies in commonly used browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

You can also opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Please note: Disabling cookies may affect your browsing experience on our site and on partner casino websites. In particular, disabling affiliate tracking cookies means that if you sign up at a partner casino after clicking our link, your referral may not be correctly recorded — which does not affect your registration or gameplay, but may affect our ability to earn commission.

7.Your Rights Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988

AussiePokies96 operates in compliance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) contained in Schedule 1 of that Act. These principles govern how Australian businesses handle personal information.

Under the Privacy Act and the APPs, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right of Access (APP 12): You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you. We will respond to access requests within a reasonable time (generally 30 days).
  • Right to Correction (APP 13): If you believe that personal information we hold about you is inaccurate, out of date, incomplete, or misleading, you have the right to request that we correct it.
  • Right to Request Deletion: You may request that we delete personal information we hold about you, subject to any legal obligations we may have to retain certain records.
  • Right to Opt Out of Marketing: Although we do not currently send marketing communications, you have the right to withdraw any consent you have provided and to request that we not use your information for marketing purposes.
  • Right to Complain: If you believe we have handled your personal information in a way that breaches the Privacy Act or the APPs, you have the right to make a complaint to us first. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at www.oaic.gov.au or by calling 1300 363 992.

To exercise any of your privacy rights, contact us at: editor@ssusa.co. Please include your name and enough detail for us to identify the information you are referring to. We will respond promptly and in accordance with our obligations under the Privacy Act.
