4.4 Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We use cookies to ensure our website functions correctly, to understand how it is used, and to support affiliate tracking.

Types of Cookies We Use

Session Cookies

These are temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. They allow our website to remember your actions and preferences during a single browsing session (such as your navigation path through the site). They are essential for the basic operation of the website and do not store any personal identifying information.

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to collect aggregated, anonymised data about how visitors use our website. Google Analytics sets cookies (such as _ga and _gid) on your device to distinguish unique users and sessions. We have enabled IP anonymisation, which means Google truncates your IP address before storing it. The data collected includes pages viewed, time on site, traffic sources, and device information. This information is processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Affiliate Tracking Cookies

When you click a link to a partner casino on our website, the casino operator or their affiliate programme may set tracking cookies on your device. These cookies record that you arrived at the casino via our website and allow us to receive commission if you register and/or make a qualifying deposit. These cookies are set by the third-party casino or affiliate network — not by AussiePokies96 directly — and are subject to the privacy policies of those third parties. We have no control over the cookies set by external casino websites.

How to Manage and Disable Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies currently stored on your device
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies (not recommended for full site functionality)

Guidance for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Apple Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Please note: If you disable cookies entirely, some features of our website may not function correctly. In particular, disabling third-party cookies may mean that affiliate tracking cannot occur, and our partner casinos may not be able to attribute your registration to our referral.
