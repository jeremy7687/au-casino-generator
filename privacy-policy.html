Section 4

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. We use the following types of cookies on AussiePokies96:

4.1 Strictly Necessary / Session Cookies

These cookies are essential for the basic functioning of the website. They are temporary and expire when you close your browser. They do not collect personally identifiable information and cannot be disabled without affecting website functionality.

4.2 Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to collect aggregated, anonymised data about how visitors use our site. Google Analytics uses cookies to track metrics such as session duration, pages viewed, and traffic sources. We have enabled IP anonymisation within Google Analytics, meaning your full IP address is never stored by Google on our behalf. The data collected is statistical in nature and does not identify you personally.

  • For more information, visit: Google's Privacy Policy
  • To opt out of Google Analytics across all websites: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

4.3 Affiliate Tracking Cookies

When you click a link to a partner casino on our website, the casino operator may set a tracking cookie on your device. This cookie enables the casino to attribute your visit or registration to our website so that we receive the appropriate affiliate commission. These cookies are set by the third-party casino operator — not by us — and are governed by that casino's own privacy policy. We do not have access to or control over these cookies once they are set.

4.4 How to Manage or Disable Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies that have already been set
  • Block cookies from specific websites or all websites
  • Set your browser to notify you when a cookie is being set

Guidance for common browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Apple Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Please note: Disabling or blocking affiliate tracking cookies may mean that affiliate referrals from our site to partner casinos cannot be tracked. This does not affect your ability to access or use partner casinos, but it may affect whether we receive commission for referring you.

Your Rights Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988

The Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) govern the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information in Australia. Under this legislation, you have the following rights in relation to your personal information:

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you. We will respond to access requests within a reasonable time.

Right to Correction

Right to Deletion

Right to Complain

If you believe your privacy rights have been breached, you have the right to make a complaint to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).

You have the right to know what personal information we hold about you and how it is used, which this policy aims to clearly explain.
