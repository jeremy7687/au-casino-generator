Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 April 2026  ·  Applies to: ssusa.co

§ 1

Who We Are

AussiePokies96 (we, us, our) is an independent online casino review and comparison website operated at ssusa.co. The site is authored and maintained by Blake Donovan.

We are an affiliate website. This means we provide independent editorial content — reviews, guides, and comparisons — about online casinos that accept Australian players. We earn a commission when a user registers at a partner casino via our referral links. We do not own, operate, license, or regulate any casino or gambling platform. Our editorial content is independent of any commercial relationship.

Important: If you have questions about your account, deposits, withdrawals, or gameplay, you must contact the casino operator directly. We cannot access your casino account or personal data held by any casino.
§ 2

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum personal information necessary to operate this website effectively and respond to user enquiries. We collect data in two ways:

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Contact form submissions: If you send us a message via our contact page or email us directly at editor@ssusa.co, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message.
  • Email correspondence: Any emails you send us are retained for the purpose of responding to your enquiry.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, certain technical information is collected automatically by our hosting infrastructure and analytics tools:

  • IP address (anonymised before storage — see Section 4)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring URLs (the page you came from)
  • Date and time of your visit
  • Clicks on affiliate links (tracked via affiliate platform cookies)
What we do NOT collect: We do not collect, store, or process any financial information, payment card details, bank account numbers, or gambling account credentials. We have no access to your accounts at any casino.
§ 3

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate and improve the website: Understanding how users navigate our site helps us improve content, structure, and usability.
  • To respond to your enquiries: If you contact us, we use your name and email to send you a reply.
  • To track affiliate referrals: When you click a link to a partner casino, affiliate tracking cookies record that referral so we can receive our commission. This does not involve us receiving any personal data about your activities on the casino's platform.
  • To analyse website traffic: We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns, popular content, and user behaviour. This helps us create better content for our readers.
  • To maintain website security: Server logs help us detect and respond to suspicious activity or technical issues.
We do not: sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for their own marketing purposes. We do not use your data for automated profiling or individual decision-making that produces legal or significant effects.

Our legal basis for processing personal data under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 is our legitimate interest in operating a functional, high-quality website, and — where you contact us — your consent in initiating that communication.

§ 4

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We use the following types of cookies:

4.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These session cookies are required for basic website functionality (e.g., navigating between pages, maintaining your session). They are deleted when you close your browser and do not collect personal information.

4.2 Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymised, aggregated data about how visitors use our website. We have enabled IP anonymisation — your IP address is truncated before it is stored, meaning Google does not retain your full IP address. Google Analytics sets cookies (typically prefixed _ga, _gid) that persist for up to 2 years. Google's privacy policy applies to data processed by their systems: policies.google.com/privacy.

4.3 Affiliate Tracking Cookies

When you click a link to a partner casino on our site, the casino's website may set its own cookies to track that referral. These cookies are set by the third-party casino, not by us. They allow the casino's affiliate system to attribute your registration to AussiePokies96 so we can receive our commission. We do not receive any personal data from these cookies — only an anonymised confirmation that a referral occurred.

4.4 Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Below are links to cookie management guides for common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
Please note: If you disable cookies, some aspects of the website may not function as intended. Disabling affiliate cookies means that if you click through to a partner casino, we may not receive our referral commission — however, this has no effect on your ability to register with or use that casino.

§ 6

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law. Our specific retention periods are:

  • Analytics data (Google Analytics): Retained for 26 months per the default Google Analytics data retention setting. After this period, data is automatically deleted from Google's servers.
  • Contact form submissions and email correspondence: Messages and associated contact details are retained for 12 months from the date of last correspondence. If your enquiry results in an ongoing relationship (e.g., a business partnership), relevant data may be retained for the duration of that relationship plus a reasonable period thereafter.
  • Server access logs: Automatically generated server logs (including IP addresses) are retained for a maximum of 90 days for security and diagnostic purposes, then permanently deleted.
  • Affiliate tracking records: Anonymised referral confirmation records may be retained for up to 24 months for commission reconciliation purposes.

When data reaches the end of its retention period, it is securely deleted or permanently anonymised. You may request early deletion of your data at any time (see Section 7).

§ 7

Your Rights Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988

The Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) govern how we handle your personal information. As an individual whose personal data we hold, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access (APP 12): You may request access to the personal information we hold about you. We will respond within 30 days of receiving a valid request.
  • Right to Correction (APP 13): If you believe personal information we hold about you is inaccurate, incomplete, or out of date, you may request that we correct it.
  • Right to Deletion: You may request that we delete personal information we hold about you. We will comply unless we are required to retain it by law or for a legitimate and proportionate purpose.
  • Right to Opt Out of Direct Marketing: If we ever send you any direct marketing communications (we currently do not), you have the right to opt out at any time.
  • Right to Make a Complaint: If you believe we have mishandled your personal information, you have the right to make a complaint to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au or by calling 1300 363 992.
To exercise any of your privacy rights, contact us at:
editor@ssusa.co
Please include your name and a clear description of your request. We will respond within 30 days.

We will not charge you a fee for making a privacy request. We may need to verify your identity before we can action your request to protect against unauthorised access to your data.
