Section 04

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. AussiePokies96 uses cookies to deliver analytics and to enable affiliate tracking. Below we explain the types of cookies used and your options.

Session Cookies

These are temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. They are used to maintain the coherence of your session as you navigate between pages on our site. No personally identifiable information is stored in these cookies.

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics (provided by Google LLC) to collect aggregated, anonymised data about how visitors use our website. This includes page views, session duration, bounce rate, and traffic sources. We have configured Google Analytics with IP anonymisation enabled, meaning the last octet of your IP address is masked before any data is stored. The data collected is statistical in nature and does not identify you personally. Google's data processing is governed by Google's Privacy Policy (available at policies.google.com/privacy). Google may process this data on servers located outside Australia.

Affiliate Tracking Cookies

When you click an affiliate link on our site and are redirected to a partner casino, that casino's website may set its own tracking cookies on your device. These cookies allow the casino to recognise that you arrived via our referral, so that we can be credited with any qualifying commission. These cookies are set by the third-party casino — not by AussiePokies96 — and are governed by each casino's own privacy policy.

Managing cookies: You can control and disable cookies through your browser settings. Most modern browsers allow you to block all cookies, accept only certain types, or delete existing cookies. Please refer to your browser's help documentation for instructions. Disabling cookies will not prevent you from using our website, but it may mean that affiliate tracking is disrupted and we are unable to be credited for referring you to a partner casino.

Opt-out of Google Analytics: You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. The following retention periods apply:

  • Analytics data (Google Analytics): Retained for 26 months in accordance with Google Analytics default data retention settings. After this period, user-level and event-level data is automatically deleted.

Once data reaches the end of its retention period, it is securely deleted or irreversibly anonymised. If you request deletion of your data earlier, we will action this in accordance with our obligations under the Australian Privacy Act 1988.
