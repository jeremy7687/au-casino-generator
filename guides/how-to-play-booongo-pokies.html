Booongo Hold & Win Explained

Hold & Win is Booongo's signature bonus mechanic and the core reason this provider has built such a loyal following among Australian punters. The mechanic originated in the broader iGaming industry but Booongo has refined it into one of the most polished implementations available in online pokies. Here's exactly how it works and how to get the most from it.

🪙 How It Triggers

Landing 6 or more special coin symbols (usually bright gold or coloured coins) in a single spin triggers the Hold & Win bonus round. Each coin symbol locks into its position on the grid while the remaining reels respin. New coins that land also lock, and the feature continues until no new coins appear on a respin.

💰 Coin Values & Jackpots

Every coin symbol carries a fixed cash value ranging from small multipliers up to jackpot prizes. Booongo implements four jackpot tiers: Mini, Minor, Major, and Grand. The Grand jackpot is awarded when all positions on the Hold & Win grid are filled — this is the game's peak payout event.
