Booongo Hold & Win Explained

Hold & Win is Booongo's most popular and recognisable mechanic — the engine behind their highest-paying titles. Understanding how it works is the key to maximising your returns on Australian online pokies from this studio.

How the Mechanic Works

Hold & Win is triggered when a minimum number of coin or special symbols land simultaneously across the reels — typically 6 or more on a standard 5×3 grid. Once activated, the base reels are replaced by a respin board: all triggering coin symbols are locked in place, and you receive three free respins. Any new coin symbols that land during a respin are also locked, and the respin counter resets to three. The feature ends when either the respin counter reaches zero or the entire grid is filled with coin symbols — the latter triggering the maximum jackpot payout.

Each coin symbol carries a cash value, and the board typically includes four jackpot tiles: Mini, Minor, Major, and Grand (or Mega). Landing a jackpot tile during respins awards that fixed prize on top of accumulated coin values. This structure means a single Hold & Win trigger can range from a modest 20× win to a life-changing 5,000× hit if the Grand jackpot is collected on a full board.
