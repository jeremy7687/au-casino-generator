Booongo Hold & Win Explained

Hold & Win is Booongo's signature mechanic — and understanding it is the key to maximising your sessions on their best titles.

Hold & Win is a bonus mechanic that was popularised in the mid-2010s and has become the defining feature of Booongo's catalogue. At its core, the mechanic works by replacing the standard free spins round with a re-spin system based on collecting special "coin" or "money" symbols on the reels. When a threshold number of these symbols land in a single spin (typically six or more, depending on the game), the Hold & Win feature is triggered.

Once triggered, the reels lock and you receive three re-spins. Every coin symbol that landed to trigger the feature stays locked in place. On subsequent re-spins, if additional coin symbols land, they also lock and your re-spin counter resets to three. The feature continues until either you run out of re-spins, or you fill the entire grid with coin symbols — the latter triggering the coveted Grand Jackpot. Each coin symbol displays a monetary value (typically ranging from 1x to 500x your bet) and sometimes includes Mini, Minor, Major, and Grand fixed jackpot prizes.
