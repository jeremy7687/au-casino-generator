Reel Power vs Paylines — Aristocrat's Unique System

One of Aristocrat's most significant contributions to pokie design is the Reel Power mechanic — a departure from fixed paylines that changed how Australians think about bet sizing and win potential. Understanding this system is essential for any punter playing Aristocrat titles online.

Traditional pokies pay on fixed lines (e.g. 20 or 25 paylines) that you activate by choosing how many to include in each spin. Reel Power abandons this entirely: instead of lines, symbols simply need to appear on adjacent reels from left to right, regardless of their vertical position. This means every possible combination across selected reels is always active — there are no deactivated lines and no missed wins due to a symbol landing on the "wrong" row.
