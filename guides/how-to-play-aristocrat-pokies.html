What Is Aristocrat?

Aristocrat Leisure Limited was founded in Sydney in 1953 by Len Ainsworth, making it one of the oldest and most storied gaming companies in the world. Listed on the ASX, the company grew from manufacturing mechanical poker machines for Australian pubs and clubs into a global powerhouse with operations across more than 200 countries. Today, Aristocrat employs over 7,500 people worldwide and generates billions in annual revenue, cementing its place as Australia's biggest and most recognisable pokies brand.

Reel Power vs Paylines: Aristocrat's Unique Betting System

One of Aristocrat's most distinctive innovations is the Reel Power system — a fundamentally different way to structure wins compared to traditional fixed paylines. Understanding this mechanic is essential before you stake real money, because it directly affects how you set your bet size.

In a traditional pokie, you choose how many paylines to activate (e.g. 1 to 25 lines) and bet a set amount per line. In Reel Power titles, all positions on adjacent reels count as winning combinations — wins pay left to right across any adjacent symbol, regardless of row. This eliminates missed wins from inactive lines but changes how you calculate your total stake.
