🎁 Types of Casino Bonuses Explained

Not all casino bonuses work the same way. Here's what each bonus type means, what terms apply, and which type of punter it suits best.

🆓

No Deposit Bonus

A no deposit bonus lets you play real money pokies without funding your account — the casino credits you with free cash or free spins just for signing up. It's the lowest-risk way to trial an offshore casino before committing your own funds.

Typical Terms: $10–$50 free cash or 10–50 free spins. Max cashout $50–$200. Wagering 25x–50x bonus.

Best for: New punters wanting to trial pokies risk-free.
💰

Welcome Deposit Bonus

A welcome deposit bonus matches your first deposit by a set percentage — commonly 100% to 400% — up to a maximum AUD amount. This is the largest bonus category available at Australian offshore casinos and often spans multiple deposits.

Typical Terms: 100%–400% match. Up to $1,000–$20,000 total. Wagering 30x–50x deposit + bonus.

Best for: Punters ready to make an initial deposit and want maximum value.
🌀

Free Spins

Free spins give you a set number of bonus rounds on specific pokies titles — often Pragmatic Play or NetEnt games — without wagering your own money. They may be awarded on registration, on deposit, or as part of ongoing promotions.

Typical Terms: 10–600 spins. Fixed coin value ($0.10–$1.00). Winnings subject to wagering 25x–45x.

Best for: Pokies lovers wanting to extend play on specific game titles.
🔄

Reload Bonus

A reload bonus rewards existing punters who make additional deposits after their initial welcome offer. These typically offer smaller match percentages but can be claimed weekly or monthly, making them ideal for regulars who want ongoing value.

Typical Terms: 25%–100% match. $500–$2,000 max. Weekly or monthly frequency. Wagering 30x–40x.

Best for: Loyal punters who play regularly and want continued rewards.
