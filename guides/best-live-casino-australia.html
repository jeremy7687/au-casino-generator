Instead of a computer-generated random number generator (RNG) determining the outcome of each hand or spin, an actual human dealer shuffles physical cards, spins a real roulette wheel, or draws tiles in a baccarat shoe. You interact via a chat interface, place your bets on a digital overlay and watch the results unfold in real time with zero delay.
