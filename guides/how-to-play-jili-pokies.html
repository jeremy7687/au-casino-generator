How JILI Free Games Are Triggered

The most common trigger mechanism across JILI pokies is the scatter symbol — typically landing 3 or more anywhere on the reels. However, JILI layers additional complexity on top of this foundation. In Super Ace, the free games system is card-rank based: each spin reveals card values (2 through Ace), and landing enough Ace symbols fills a bonus meter that triggers the feature. This means even non-winning spins contribute to the bonus progress — a mechanic that keeps punters engaged across extended sessions.

Golden Empire takes a different approach with its cascading reel engine. When scatter symbols appear during the base game, they award a cascade trigger — winning symbol clusters are removed and replaced by falling symbols from above. Each cascade during free spins adds to a multiplier that persists throughout the feature, meaning a long cascade chain can result in a single spin paying out at 5x, 10x, or even higher multipliers. This is the mechanic that drives Golden Empire's 5,000x max win potential.

Lock-and-Spin & Hold-and-Win Mechanics

Several JILI titles — particularly JILI Caishen and Money Coming — use a hold-and-win (lock-and-spin) mechanic rather than traditional free spins. Here, special coin symbols land on the reels and lock in place while the remaining reels re-spin. Each re-spin that lands another coin symbol resets the counter to 3. The feature ends when you run out of re-spins or fill all positions — with a full board triggering a grand jackpot.
