What Is a No KYC Casino?

KYC stands for Know Your Customer — a regulatory process requiring casinos to verify a punter's identity before processing withdrawals. At standard casinos, this involves uploading government-issued ID, a utility bill, and sometimes a bank statement. Offshore casinos operating under a Curaçao e-Gaming licence are not bound by AUSTRAC's domestic AML/CTF reporting obligations in the same way that locally licensed operators would be.
