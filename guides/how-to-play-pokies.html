What Are Online Pokies?

The definitive AU-English breakdown — history, why Aussies are obsessed, and how online versions differ from the pub classics.

Online pokies are digital reel-based casino games where punters spin to match symbols across paylines or clusters to win payouts. The term "pokie" is uniquely Australian and New Zealand — it's short for "poker machine," tracing back to the video poker and fruit machines that flooded pubs and clubs across Australia from the 1970s onward. Today, Australians hold one of the highest per-capita gambling participation rates in the world, and pokies — both physical and online — sit at the centre of that culture.

Online pokies replicate and dramatically improve on their physical counterparts. How Pokies Work

Four core mechanics every punter needs to understand before placing a single spin — ignore these and you're playing blind.

📊

RTP — Return to Player

RTP is the theoretical percentage of all wagered money a pokie pays back to punters over millions of spins. A 96% RTP means for every $100 wagered, the game returns $96 on average long-term. Most quality online pokies sit between 94–97% RTP. Always check the paytable — anything below 94% is worth avoiding.

Aim for 96%+
📈

Volatility

Volatility (also called variance) describes how a pokie pays out. Low volatility = small, frequent wins — good for stretching a small bankroll. Medium = balanced. High volatility = rare but big wins — suits punters chasing big jackpots with patience. Gates of Olympus and Sweet Bonanza are high volatility; Fire Joker is medium.

Match to bankroll
🔢

Paylines & Ways to Win

Traditional pokies use fixed paylines (10, 20, 25) — symbols must land left to right on active lines to pay. Modern formats include 243 Ways (any adjacent reels count), Cluster Pays (groups of matching symbols), and Megaways (dynamic reels offering up to 117,649 ways per spin). More ways generally means more frequent, smaller hits.

Megaways = most ways
⚡

Bonus Features

Modern pokies are built around bonus features: Free Spins triggered by scatter symbols, Multipliers that amplify wins (sometimes 100x+), Sticky Wilds that hold on reels for multiple spins, Bonus Buy to purchase direct access to the feature round, and Cascading Reels where winning symbols explode and refill for chain reactions.

Free spins = key feature
💡 RTP in Practice

RTP is calculated over millions of spins — your individual session will vary wildly from the published percentage. A 96.5% RTP pokie can still drain your bankroll in 20 spins or pay 500x in one bonus round. Use RTP as a selection filter (higher is better), not as a session-by-session guarantee. Pair high RTP with low volatility if you want longer play sessions on a set budget.
