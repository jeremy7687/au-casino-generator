What Are Online Pokies?

The Australian term, the global product, and why punters Down Under are obsessed.

How Pokies Work

Understand RTP, volatility, paylines and bonus features before you stake a cent.

Core Concept
📊
Return to Player (RTP)
RTP is the theoretical percentage of all wagered money a pokie pays back over millions of spins. A pokie with 96% RTP returns $96 for every $100 wagered — across a massive sample. In a single session your results will deviate wildly, but choosing high-RTP titles (96%+) gives you the best long-run odds. Anything under 94% is worth avoiding unless the bonus features are exceptional. Always check the RTP in the game's info panel before spinning with real money.
Risk Level
🎯
Volatility (Variance)
Volatility describes how a pokie distributes wins. Low volatility pokies pay small amounts frequently — ideal for stretching your bankroll and grinding through wagering requirements. High volatility pokies pay rarely but hit big when they do — suited to punters chasing large multiplier wins with a defined risk budget. Medium volatility is the sweet spot for most sessions: balanced hit frequency with solid win potential. Gates of Olympus and Sweet Bonanza are high-volatility favourites.
Win Structure
⚡
Paylines & Ways to Win
Traditional pokies used fixed paylines (9, 25, 50) — you win when matching symbols land left to right on active lines. Modern formats include Cluster Pays (groups of 5+ matching symbols anywhere), Megaways (dynamic reels with up to 117,649 ways to win per spin), and 243-ways all-pays formats. Megaways titles like Wanted Dead or a Wild deliver huge hit potential but demand higher bankroll patience due to elevated volatility.
Features
🎁
Bonus Features
Modern pokies are built around bonus features that drive variance and excitement. Key ones to know: Free Spins (triggered by scatter symbols, often with multipliers); Sticky Wilds (wilds that remain on reels for multiple spins); Multipliers (boost wins 2x–500x+); Buy Bonus (pay to enter the bonus round instantly — available on many offshore sites); and Cascading Reels where winning symbols disappear and new ones fall in. Understanding these before you spin helps you pick the right title for your session goals.
💡 Pro Tip: RNG Certification

Every reputable offshore pokie is powered by a certified Random Number Generator (RNG) — audited by independent labs like eCOGRA, iTech Labs, or BMM. This means no spin can be predicted or manipulated. The house edge comes purely from the built-in RTP, not from any tampering.
