RTP (Return to Player)

RTP is the percentage of all wagered money a pokie pays back over millions of spins. A 96% RTP means — on average — $96 returns for every $100 wagered. The casino keeps the remaining 4% (house edge). Online pokies typically run 94–97% RTP, significantly higher than land-based machines (often 85–88%).

Paylines & Ways to Win

Classic pokies have fixed paylines (e.g. 20 lines). Modern variants use different win mechanics:

  • Fixed Paylines — traditional left-to-right combinations
  • 243 / 1024 Ways — any position on adjacent reels wins
  • Megaways™ — up to 117,649 ways to win per spin (dynamic reels)
  • Cluster Pays — groups of matching symbols pay, no lines needed
